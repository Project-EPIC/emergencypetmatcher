Welcome to EmergencyPetMatcher, digital volunteer!

EPM is a web site focused on bringing volunteers to report, match, and verify lost and found pets, especially those misplaced by their owners during disaster events. Pets are important household members, and their displacement affects the emotional welfare of families. During the 2005 Hurricane Katrina, for example, estimates have shown that 70,000 pets were misplaced from their owners, and only around 2% of them were reunited.

The EPM team will be constantly engineering, testing, and fine-tuning the system so that it is easy to use and valuable for reuniting pets with their owners. On behalf of all of us at the Project EPIC (Empowering the Public with Information in Crisis) team at the University of Colorado at Boulder, thank you for your contributions!
